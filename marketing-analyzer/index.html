How we score click to expand — good for a first read

Composite score

Composite = 0.4 × rule + 0.6 × AI. The single number you sort and compare on. Range 0–100.

Rule score (0–100)

Automatic technical checks on the landing page's HTML: HTTPS, Open Graph tags, schema.org markup, language tags, CTA copy like "Plan een bezichtiging", image alt text, contact links, word count.

AI score (0–100)

A senior-reviewer judgement on 8 dimensions, averaged to 100. 5/10 = Amsterdam market average. 8+ is rare. 2 or below = visibly broken or empty.
